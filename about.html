About Computer Science Department Evening

Computer Science department evening has been established in the academic year of 2017 - 2018. It becomes the first department at College of Science in University of Garmian. This department has 30 students from all over the country with exceptional teaching staff with high experienced in the field.

Our missions:

  1. Provide excellent education facility to undergraduate student so they can fulfil society needs and led our country to the highest technological level.
  2. Conducting research to advance the state of the art in computer science and to solve the real world problem of society.
  3. Provide university staff with exceptional training courses to develop their computer skills and improve quality of their achievements.
  4. Holding scientific seminars, workshops and conferences to improve quality of university staff and to advance their scientific knowledge.

Vision:

  1. Our vision is to provide the best possible environment to conduct research and prepare students for the fast paced technological advances.

Objectives:

  1. Graduated student have the highest knowledge and skill so they can purse career in industry or continuing their education towards higher education.
  2. The department stays at the standard level of teaching.
  3. The department always make sure that curriculum is up-to-date with the advancement in the field.
  4. Making sure that we provide best facilities for teaching and researching.
  5. We are well known and respected in the regional community.
